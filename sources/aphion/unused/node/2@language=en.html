Our Services

Featured projects

  • Cloudbeam

    Digital Home Control iOS App

    Client: SkyCentrics
    Domain: Digital Home
    Technology: iOS, C++, PHP
    SkyCentrics (formerly known as Cloudbeam Inc.) has been our regular client since 2008. We developed multiple applications for remote management of Digital Home devices (thermostats, cameras, irrigation equipment and other specific devices) via iPhone/iPad/iPod.

  • Magnate - Build Your Monopoly for iOS

    Multi-player Real-time Game

    Client: Mobe Labb
    Domain: Games
    Technology: iOS (iPhone, iPad), Cocos2D-x
    Magnate is a multi-player real-time game. The game combines the principles of a classic Monopoly board game and strategic thinking. CactusSoft created iOS and Android versions of the game as well as the version for desktops.

  • Wars Online for iOS

    Strategic 2D Arcade Game

    Client: Mobe Labb
    Domain: Games
    Technology: iOS (iPhone / iPad)
    Wars Online is a real-time casual game with multi- and single-player modes. We worked on the project through all the stages: from creating a plot and characters to software engineering, testing and implementation.

  • Backup Assistant

    VZ Contact Transfer App for iOS

    Client:Verizon
    Domain: Productivity
    Technology: iOS
    The application for Verizon Wireless’ users transfers contacts from the Backup Assistant device to an iPhone.
