About Us

CactusSoft is a full cycle custom software development company providing software development services internationally. Since the foundation in 2007, the company has been working with clients located in the USA, Europe and Russia.

Among our clients there are: Nokia, Yandex (Russia), Odnoklassniki (Russia), Wargaming.net, Danish Broadcasting Corporation, Leo Burnett, Yves Rocher, Adzuki Mobile, Turner Broadcasting (USA), Synchronoss (USA) and others.

The company's services cover a complete software product life cycle: from UI design to software development, testing, and integration. Our engineers have successfully realized dozens of complex software projects across several industries solving a range of tough business challenges.

The company's development practices are based on agility, continuous integration and ongoing communication. CactusSoft's team is located in Belarus, Eastern Europe. The company is a resident of Belarus' High Technologies Park (BHTP). The IT infrastructure in Belarus operates by the international standards, so does our company.
