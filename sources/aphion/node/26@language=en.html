  • WISP

    WISP is a mobile-first Intranet solution for creating and managing mobile employee handbooks. It helps business owners effectively communicate important info about their companies to the employees.

    WISP can be used for various HR-related activities: from quick new employee onboarding and adaptation to developing the current staff members’ deeper understanding of the company’s processes.

    WISP features:

    • Employees can check the company’s policy at the touch of a finger.
    • All the staff members’ contacts are in one place: phone numbers, e-mail addresses, Skype IDs, and office affiliations.
    • The company news sharing option helps keep employees updated about everything that happens in the company.
    • Corporate messenger can be used for quick work communication.

    The following technologies were used to develop the WISP web interface:

    • Front-end: AngularJS, the combination of Bower, Grunt, and Yeoman.
    • Back-end: ASP.NET WebApi, OWIN, Katana, MongoDB.

    The WISP apps are available for iOS and Android smartphone users. The apps require iOS 7 or later, and Android 4.0.3 or later. To create the apps, CactusSoft mobile development team used the following technologies:

    • For Android: Jackson, Glide, Dagger2.
    • For iOS: AFNetworking, AutoScrollLabel, SDWebImage.

    To start using WISP, an employer has to register on the service’s website, add all the necessary information, and invite employees to download WISP app for iOS or Android. The apps are available to download for free on iTunes and Google Play.

    wisp_employee handbook

  • Propeller for Android

    About the project:

    The application is developed for propeller-app.com. The basic idea of the project was to develop an app which could provide an easy and fast way to become virtually acquainted with people whom you saw and liked but did not have the courage to come up and introduce yourself.

    With a certain time interval the app scans the area (the closest proximity of 50m/150ft) looking for devices with the same app being active at the moment. Once discovered, the devices with Propeller on them connect and exchange user information. The data is logged and saved. Users can browse through the profiles of other users whom the app has ‘met’, and then contact them at any time using Internet connection.

    Technology:

    CactusSoft mobile development team used Android and a multipeer connectivity framework technologies to develop the app. Propeller utilizes the encoded Wi-Fi channel and does not use GPS or cellular networks.

    Benefits:

    The application is aimed at making it easy to meet new people and make friends. It works in the background and does not drain the battery. A user profile contains only a photo; the name and age parameters are optional.
